Recipe #1 Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
1.All-purpose flour 1.5 cups
2.Baking powder 3.5 teaspoons
3.White sugar 1 teaspoons
4.Egg 1
5.Milk 1 cup

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Recipe #2 Chinese Pork Dumplings

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
1.Ground pork 1 pround
2.Large egg, beaten 1
3.Dumpling wrappers 50
4.Finely chopped Chinese chives 2 tablespoons
5.Soy sauce 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Prepare dumplings: Mix pork, garlic, egg, chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.

Step 2: Place a dumpling wrapper on a lightly floured work surface and spoon about 1 tablespoon of the filling in the middle. Wet the edge with a little water and crimp together forming small pleats to seal the dumpling. Repeat to form remaining dumplings.

Step 3: Heat 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place 8 to 10 dumplings in the pan and cook until browned, about 2 minutes per side. Pour in 1 cup of water; cover and cook until the dumplings are tender and pork is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
